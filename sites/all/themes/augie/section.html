An Education of Enduring Worth

Students who enroll at Augustana are attracted by our intellectually challenging yet supportive learning environment, the integration of faith, and an active and engaged campus community. These elements of the Augustana experience add up to an education of enduring worth, preparing you for a lifetime of learning and service.

We invite you to consider becoming part of the Augustana community. Learn more about Augustana by browsing these pages, talking with one of our admission counselors, or better yet, by visiting our campus. We hope to hear from you soon!
